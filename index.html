About Project

For my term project, I am developing a Personalized and Sustainable Shopping Platform designed to enhance the online shopping experience while promoting environmentally friendly practices. The platform uses advanced algorithms to curate personalized product recommendations based on user preferences, past purchases, and sustainability criteria.

Key features include a Personalization Engine that leverages machine learning to tailor suggestions to individual tastes, and Sustainability Filters that allow users to search for products based on attributes like eco-friendly materials and ethical sourcing. The user-friendly interface prioritizes easy navigation and access to detailed sustainability information for each product.

Additionally, the platform encourages community engagement through user reviews and shared experiences, creating a supportive network of environmentally conscious shoppers. It also offers educational resources to inform users about sustainable shopping practices.

Throughout the semester, I conducted user research and usability testing to refine the platform, receiving overwhelmingly positive feedback. This project not only enhanced my understanding of e-commerce and sustainability but also provided me with valuable skills in software development and user experience design. It positions me well for future roles in tech-focused sustainability initiatives, aligning with the growing demand for responsible consumerism.
